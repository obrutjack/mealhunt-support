## English

### Privacy Policy for MealHunt

MealHunt ("the App") is developed by Chuan-Yi Liu. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

#### Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** The App stores all information (restaurants, visit records, statistics) locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

#### Location Data

The App requests location access solely to:
- Search for nearby restaurants on Apple Maps
- Calculate distances to restaurants
- Verify arrival at restaurants for check-in

Location data is processed on-device and is never transmitted to external servers.

#### Third-Party Services

The App uses the following Apple services:
- **MapKit** — for map display and restaurant search
- **WeatherKit** — for weather-based recommendations (if enabled)

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policy.

#### Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.

#### Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, all data is permanently removed.

#### Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy:
- Email: obrutjack@yahoo.com
